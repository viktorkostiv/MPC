Purpose

MPS aspires to empower individuals by allowing them to be part of a community that strives to make a difference. We seek to support causes such as land preservation, wildlife rehabilitation, animal activism, humanitarian aid, and conservation of endangered species. We hope that MPS NFTs can open a discussion and inspire people to realize how beautiful, weird and wonderful our world truly is and that it’s a gift that should be cherished.

Story

The  Metaverse  Jungle was a haven for the unique. Across the dense canopies spread greenery and waterways enveloped in a sea of creatures. From the smallest of amphibians to birds akin to a kaleidoscope, dawn brought with it a cacophony of life.

As usual, the air would swirl and gather its load of water by evening, drenching those below in a dramatic downpour, yet on this day, the morning calm was interrupted by a blistering shriek, accompanied by a flash so bright even the nocturnal residents were awoken. 

The hustle and bustle of daily jungle life were paused, as all creatures gazed upwards with a mixture of astonishment and fear, only to be welcomed by a light show of cosmic dust and noise. Eventually, the frozen rock carved its path through the sky in a fireball, landing amid the dense  Metaverse jungle.

As the quiet subsided, a clan of parrots were amongst the first to brave into the unknown. Ten, in particular, were guided in awe to its resting place, witness to the pulsating glow of the rock, clearly something not of their world. As they gazed on, they would quickly realize a change had taken place inside them, a transformation of sorts they had no idea of what lay in store for the  Metaverse.
